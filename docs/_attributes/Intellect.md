> This attribute measures the mathematical, conceptual, and deductive capabilities of a character. Typical skills which it could govern include estimation (mentally figuring out values), deciphering languages, or code-breaking.

No Attribute
------------

Intellect can be replaced by the [Know-how](Know-how) skill.

Notes
-----

Intellect is a D6 [Attribute](index#attributes).
