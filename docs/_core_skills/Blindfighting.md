__Time Required:__ 5 seconds

__Description:__ A character with _blindfighting_ can operate or battle in dark or otherwise reduced-sight conditions (blizzards, blindfolded, and so forth). (Characters in combat normally suffer a -2D penalty during full darkness conditiosns. See Chapter Eight: Combat for more information.) The character must make a _blindfighting_ roll every 5 seconds to continue to enjoy the skill's effect.

Ability                  | Effect                      | Difficulty
------------------------ | --------------------------- | ----------
Sense location of being  | eliminate blindness penalty | Moderate
Locate inanimate objects | ---                         | Difficult
