__Time Required:__ 5 seconds

__Description:__ A character who possesses this skill knows the history of a particular area, whether it be as small as a city or as large as an entire world.

Area of Knowledge          | Difficulty
-------------------------- | ----------
Timeline of major events   | Very Easy
Nations, leaders, and wars | Moderate
Legends and myths          | Difficult
Ancient occurences         | Very Difficult

Increase the difficulty depending upon the size of the area involved.

Size of Area  | Increase Difficulty By
------------- | ----------------------
City          | ---
Sub-continent | 1 level
Continent     | 2 levels
World         | 3 levels
Sector        | 4 levels
