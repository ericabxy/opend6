__Role:__ To secure a safe journey, pilots spend a great deal of time mapping out their intended routes. Skills in navigation and cartography as well as knowledge of the constellation improve their chaces of success in these endeavors. The pilot may choose which type of vessel---seafaring, ice-faring, sky-faring, space-faring, or time-faring.
