__Role:__ The spelljacker tracks and steals all manner of enchanted objects, from weapons to spellgems to lost artifacts. These wizards study various aspects of magic as well as the subtle nuances of infiltration and theft. They employ many skills in their quest to capture the items of their (or their master's) desire.
