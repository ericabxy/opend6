__Time Required:__ 5 seconds.

__Description:__ Characters with this skill can tumble, leap, and roll to avoid falling damage, to entertain an audience, or to surprise an opponent in combat. The difficulty required to accomplish a given feat varies with each situation. Use the following table as a guideline.

Difficulty | Action
---------- | ------
Easy       | Roll to surprise an opponent (gives +1D to attack roll with melee weapon)
Moderate   | Leap across a gap less than twice the character's height in length
Difficult  | Tumble to alleviate damage from a melee weapon (gives +2D to Endurance rolls vs. damage)
