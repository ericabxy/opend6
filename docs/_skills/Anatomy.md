__Time Required:__ 5 seconds

__Description:__ Characters with this skill have studied the main functions of the bodies of those species in the animal kingdom.

Action                                                  | Difficulty
------------------------------------------------------- | ----------
Guess main functions of a creature's visible features   | Easy
Discover creatures's weaknesses based on its appearance | Moderate
Alter healing procedures based on specific anatomy†     | Very Difficult
Diagnose infections and viruses                         | Heroic

† Adding his _anatomy_ die codes as a bonus to his _healing_ skill.
