__Time Required:__ Varies depending on local haggling customs

__Description:__ Thrifty characters often employ haggling to lower the price of good they purchase or raise the price of merchandise they wish to sell.

Alter Price By | Difficulty
:------------: | ----------
     10%       | Very Easy
     20%       | Easy
     30%       | Moderate
     40%       | Difficult
     50%       | Very Difficult
     60%       | Heroic
