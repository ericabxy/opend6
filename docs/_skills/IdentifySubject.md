__Description:__ This skill allows a character to determine whether or not a substance is lethal to his species (increase the difficulty by two levels for other species).

Action                                   | Difficulty     | Time Required
---------------------------------------- | -------------- | -------------
Determine whether substance is poisonous | Easy           | 1 minute
Determine type of poison\*               | Moderate       | 5 minutes
Determine name of poison                 | Difficult      | 10 minutes
Determine antidote                       | Very Difficult | 1 hour
======================================== | ============== | ======
\* sleep, sickness, death, etc.          |
