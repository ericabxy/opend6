__Time Required:__ 5 seconds

__Description:__ Those who dare to tread in the deep waters of lakes, rivers, and oceans must have the _swimming_ skill or will risk drowning.

Action                          | Difficulty
------------------------------- | ----------
Swim in clear, calm water       | Very Easy
Swim in choppy water            | Easy
Swim in rough waves             | Moderate
Swim during a heavy storm       | Difficult
Save another drowning character | Very Difficult
